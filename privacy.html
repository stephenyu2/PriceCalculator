Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Launch Valley Tutoring LLC ("Launch Valley Tutoring," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding the personal information you provide when visiting our website or using our services.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, student information (such as grade level and academic goals), and other information voluntarily submitted through consultation forms or enrollment materials.

Payment Information: Billing details and payment-related information processed securely through third-party payment providers.

Usage Data: Technical data such as IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and general site interaction data.

2. How We Use Your Information

To Provide Services: To deliver tutoring and academic advisory services, schedule sessions, and communicate with families.

To Process Payments: To manage billing, tuition payments, and account administration.

To Improve Our Services: To analyze usage patterns and enhance the performance and functionality of our website and programs.

To Communicate: To send service updates, scheduling confirmations, and relevant academic information. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information.

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties that assist with payment processing, scheduling systems, email communications, website hosting, and related operational functions.
  • Legal Compliance: If required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information. However, no online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

5. Your Rights

You may request:

  • Access to personal information we maintain
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of personal information, subject to legal and contractual obligations

Requests may be submitted using the contact information below.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience and analyze website traffic. You may control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our website or services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
